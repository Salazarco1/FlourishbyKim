ABOUT ME

Flowers always seem to bloom through things that should have stopped them. I understand that kind of resilience. I’m drawn to artificial florals because they stay full, bright, and intentional—just like the moments we never want to fade. Flourish by Kim is my way of taking all the growth I’ve fought for and turning it into something that lasts. I create custom florals for weddings, events, holidays, and special moments of every kind. Whether it’s elegant Christmas arrangements, festive holiday pieces, or unique florals for school dances, I love designing something that feels personal to you. My goal is simple: make beautiful, lasting pieces that match your vision and look incredible in photos for years to come
